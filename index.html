My Thoughts Film lists

The personal site of: Nicholas J. Olson

Two of the greatest things in this world are Zombie Flicks and Science Fiction. I was practically raised on them. Both genres of film have a powerful ability to observe and critique society. What's really special is that they can do everything so subtlely. Doing so without offending the majority of the populous. The films make starting a conversation about social issues easier.

Some of the societal issues are:

  • Racism
  • classism
  • sexism
  • xenophobia
  • humanism
